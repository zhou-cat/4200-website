With a background in design, Lang's creative journey was influenced by his experiences as a "Beijing drifter," navigating the challenges and aspirations of urban life.The character Hirono was developed to embody a spectrum of human emotions, including love, joy, sadness, fear, kindness, and cowardice. Through Hirono, Lang aims to highlight the subtle fluctuations of life, its ups and downs. Lang's personal narrative and artistic vision have significantly contributed to the depth and relatability of the Hirono series, resonating with collectors and art enthusiasts worldwide.
